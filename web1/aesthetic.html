Aesthetic

An aesthetic body typically refers to a physique that combines muscularity, symmetry, and low body fat to create a visually appealing and well-proportioned appearance. This look is often inspired by classic bodybuilders like Frank Zane or fitness influencers focused more on visual balance than sheer size.
