Hybird

A hybrid body is the best of both worlds — a combination of aesthetic physique, functional strength, and endurance. Think of it as the body of someone who can lift heavy, run fast, and still look jacked. This is the kind of build seen in CrossFit athletes, military special forces, MMA fighters, and hybrid trainers like David Goggins or Nick Bare.
